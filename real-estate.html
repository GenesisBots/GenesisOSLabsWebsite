Real estate on DeedOS

What this is: DeedOS is the real-estate operating layer on GenesisOS—built for brokers, agents, buyers, and sellers who need faster closings, clearer records, and optional tokenized property workflows.

Why it matters: Traditional closings drag on paperwork, wire delays, and reconciling systems that do not talk to each other. DeedOS unifies deal rooms, settlement rails, and RWA tooling in one place you can run local, in the cloud, or hybrid.

DeedOS — real estate operating system

DeedOS sits on GenesisOS and gives every party in a transaction a shared, auditable workspace—from listing through close and optional on-chain settlement.

For brokers

Pipeline visibility across offices, commission splits, and compliance checkpoints—without juggling five disconnected tools.

For agents

Offer tracking, document collection, and client updates in one flow—so buyers and sellers always know the next step.

For buyers

Transparent milestones, secure document exchange, and optional tokenized equity or fractional interest when your deal structure allows it.

For sellers

Faster proof-of-title handoffs, settlement status in plain language, and settlement rails that can finalize in seconds—not days.

Tokenized transactions & fast settlement

When your legal and compliance teams approve tokenized workflows, DeedOS can route settlement across supported chains with 3–5 second blockchain finality—replacing wire-wait anxiety with verifiable on-chain confirmation.

Speed

3–5 second settlement

Funds and title signals reconcile on-chain in seconds, with human review gates where regulations require them.

Clarity

Single deal timeline

Every party sees the same status: offer accepted, contingencies cleared, funds settled, keys released.

Control

Tokenized or traditional

Run fully traditional closings today; enable tokenized rails when your counsel and partners are ready—no rip-and-replace.

RWA tokenization

Real-world asset (RWA) workflows let you represent property interest, revenue shares, or fund units on supported networks—with audit trails tied back to DeedOS deal records.

Fractional & fund structures

Model LP interests, syndications, and co-ownership with caps, vesting, and transfer rules your legal team defines.

On-chain registry

Link deeds, liens, and token metadata so investors and operators pull from one source of truth.

Investor-ready reporting

Distributions, occupancy, and redemption events surfaced for accredited and institutional participants.
