Accessibility Statement

Last Updated: April 24, 2026

GenesisOS Labs LLC (“GenesisOS Labs”, “we”, “us”) is committed to improving accessibility on GenesisOSLabs.com for every visitor, including people who use assistive technologies, keyboard navigation, or high-contrast settings.

1. Commitment

We aim to meet common accessibility expectations for semantic HTML, readable contrast, responsive layouts, and descriptive text alternatives for meaningful images.

2. Ongoing Improvements

Accessibility is iterative. We review pages when content changes materially and welcome specific reports so we can prioritize fixes quickly.

3. Feedback

If you encounter a barrier, email support@GenesisOSLabs.com with the page URL, the assistive technology you use, and what you expected to happen. We will respond as soon as practicable.
