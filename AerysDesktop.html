Aerys Desktop v1.0.0

By GenesisOS Labs

Aerys Desktop is your local‑first Company OS — the platform where your business runs its agents, automations, workflows, business intelligence, and AI‑powered systems without relying on a dozen scattered apps. Inside Aerys, GBot Agents act as lightweight automation workers that execute repetitive tasks, monitor processes, organize operations, and keep your company’s workflows moving automatically.

Most teams drown in broken processes, manual work, and disconnected tools that never talk to each other. Aerys Desktop centralizes your operations, automates the busywork, improves visibility, & provides a scalable platform that grows with the business over time — all running directly on your machine for speed, privacy, and control of your data.

Built for founders, operators, and teams who want simpler automation, cleaner workflow management, and real operational enterprise intelligence — without needing a full engineering department to stitch everything together.
