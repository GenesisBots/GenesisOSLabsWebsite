What the Coliseum Is

The GenesisOS Coliseum is a hybrid 2D/3D battle viewer with camera choreography, stat overlays, swarm intelligence, and evolution events. It is designed for demos, tournaments, and automated testing of GBots and GSwarms.

Coliseum features

Cinematic Battles

Real-time camera pans, zooms, and holographic overlays.

Evolution Studio Integration

Trigger evolutions directly from the arena.

Swarm Intelligence

Royals, workers, and lineage-based behaviors.
