What is GJobsOS?

GJobsOS is a job‑finding operating system built inside GenesisOS — a full‑stack, AI‑powered career engine running locally, privately, and fully automated. It replaces the dozen tabs, spreadsheets, and copy‑paste rituals of a modern job search with one connected workspace.

Your resume, outreach, applications, prep, and follow‑ups all live in a single command center. GBots handle the repetitive work in the background while you focus on conversations that actually move your career forward.

Because GJobsOS runs on your machine with your own local LLM, your career data, drafts, and contact history stay yours — no third‑party career platform mining your story. Subcribe when you need it, pause when you don't!

Why GJobsOS Exists

The modern job search is broken. Candidates are told to "apply to hundreds of jobs," but the tooling has not changed in a decade — resumes are still rewritten by hand, outreach is still copy‑pasted, and every application platform tries to lock you into its walled garden. Meanwhile, AI changed everything else about work.

GJobsOS was built for the people doing the searching, not the platforms profiting from them. It treats your career like a system worth automating: structured data, programmable workflows, and a local AI brain that learns your goals instead of selling them.

Built on GenesisOS, GJobsOS gives one operator the leverage of an entire career‑services team — running quietly on your own hardware, on your terms.
